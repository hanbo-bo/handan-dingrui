Set Screw (Set Bolt)紧定螺钉

Stainless steel 304/316, Carbon steel 35/45#, Alloy steel

Product Description 产品描述

紧定螺钉（也称无头螺钉或固定螺钉）是一种无头或低轮廓螺纹紧固件，用于将一个零件固定在另一个零件上或之间。常用于将皮带轮、齿轮和手柄固定在轴上，或防止零件之间的相对运动。可选平端、锥端、柱端和凹端。

Set screws (also called grub screws or set bolts) are headless or low-profile threaded fasteners designed to secure one component within or against another. They are commonly used to fix pulleys, gears, and handles onto shafts, or to prevent relative motion between parts. Available in flat point, cone point, dog point, and cup point ends.
