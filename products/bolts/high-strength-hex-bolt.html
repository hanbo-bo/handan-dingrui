High-Strength Hex Bolt高强度外六角螺栓

Carbon steel 8.8/10.9/12.9, Alloy steel 40Cr, 35CrMo

Product Description 产品描述

高强度外六角螺栓采用合金钢制造并经过热处理，达到 8.8 级、10.9 级或 12.9 级，为苛刻的结构应用提供卓越的抗拉强度。广泛用于钢结构建筑、桥梁工程和重型设备。

High-strength hex bolts are manufactured from alloy steel and heat-treated to achieve grades 8.8, 10.9 or 12.9, delivering superior tensile strength for demanding structural applications. Used extensively in steel construction, bridge engineering, and heavy equipment where failure is not an option.
