Stainless Steel Hex Bolt不锈钢外六角螺栓

Stainless steel 201, 304, 316, 316L, A2-70, A4-80

Product Description 产品描述

不锈钢外六角螺栓具有优异的耐腐蚀性能，适用于户外、海洋、化工和食品加工环境。304 材质提供常规耐腐蚀性能，316 材质在含氯和高温环境下表现更佳。适合需要长期使用且无需表面处理的项目。

Stainless steel hex bolts offer excellent corrosion resistance for outdoor, marine, chemical and food-processing environments. Grade 304 provides general corrosion resistance, while Grade 316 delivers superior performance in chloride-rich and high-temperature conditions. Ideal for projects requiring long-term durability without surface treatment.
