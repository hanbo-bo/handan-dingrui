Company Profile

Handan Dingrui Metal Products Co., Ltd. is located in Handan, Hebei, the core base of China’s fastener industry, and is a professional manufacturer focused on the research, production, and global export of high-quality metal fasteners.

The company is committed to integrating China’s mature metal products supply chain resources, providing stable and efficient one-stop fastener solutions for global customers, and helping customers improve procurement efficiency and supply chain competitiveness.

Main products include bolts, nuts, screws, self-tapping screws, drilling screws, washers, threaded rods, anchors, stainless steel fasteners, and various non-standard custom metal parts, widely used in construction engineering, steel structures, machinery manufacturing, solar mounting systems, furniture hardware, and industrial assembly.

In addition to standardized product supply, the company provides a complete foreign trade service system, including quotation support, sample development, technical confirmation, order production follow-up, quality inspection control, export packaging design, and global logistics coordination to ensure stable delivery of every order.

Dingrui always follows the business philosophy of quality as the core, customer orientation, and long-term cooperation. Its products are exported to the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Europe, South America, Africa, and other regions, earning long-term trust from global customers.

In the future, we will continue to improve manufacturing capabilities and international service standards to become a trusted long-term fastener partner for global customers.
